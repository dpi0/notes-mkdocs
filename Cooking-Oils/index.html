    Cooking Oils

    Avoid: refined sunflower, vegetable, dalda, canola, palm oil, groundnut oil, soyabean oil due to low boiling point

    Good oil: unrefined mustard oil, organic cold press coconut oil, ghee - high boiling point

    Rice barn, sesame oil is good but never used

    soya bean oil- healthier than sunflower, and tastes as neutral.
